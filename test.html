🔧 How It Works

This page loads the built widget.js via a regular <script> tag — exactly how a customer would embed it on their website.

<script
  src="/dist/widget.js"
  data-widget-id="widget_56a2a66178452af6bae8ab07"
  data-api-url="http://localhost:3000/api/v1"
  data-ws-url="http://localhost:3002"
></script>
